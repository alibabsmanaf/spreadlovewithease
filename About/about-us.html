About Us


Our Mission: At Spread Love Organization, our mission is to provide love, care, and a bright future for orphaned and vulnerable children. We believe that every child deserves a safe and nurturing environment where they can thrive and reach their full potential.


Our Story: Our journey began 05/Feb/2023. It started with a small group of dedicated individuals who were passionate about making a difference in the lives of children facing adversity. Over the years, we have grown into a loving community that opens its doors to those in need.


What Drives Us: We are driven by a deep sense of compassion and the belief that every child, regardless of their circumstances, should have the opportunity to dream, learn, and grow. Our commitment to creating a kinder and more sustainable world extends beyond our walls.


Our Approach: At Spread Love Organization, we provide more than just shelter and food; we offer a loving home where children can heal, learn, and build lifelong relationships. Our holistic approach focuses on physical, emotional, and educational development, empowering children to become confident and responsible individuals.


Our Values:

  • Compassion: We lead with love and empathy, understanding the unique needs of each child.
  • Sustainability: We are dedicated to sustainable practices, ensuring a better future for all.
  • Community: We believe in the power of a supportive community, both within and beyond our walls.

Our Team

Meet the dedicated individuals who make Spread Love Organization a loving and nurturing environment. Our team includes caregivers, educators, counselors, and volunteers, all working together to provide the best care possible.
